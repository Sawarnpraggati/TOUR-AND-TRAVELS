FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS


Can I take just a section (Hotel/Transport/flights) of the package?

Yes, you may choose just a section of the package but we will suggest you to take whole package as its money and time saving.

How many days prior to my trip shall I book the package?

For holiday packages and custom-made tours, we strongly recommend you to book it at least 1Month prior to the travel date. For larger groups we would request you to book the package 1-2 months prior to your trip.

What Documents shall I carry during my trip???

Documents like booking Voucher/ confirmation Letter, & ID Proof must be carried by the customers during their trip.

I want to cancel my trip, How can I do it?? Do I need to pay any cancellation charges??

To cancel your trip just send a cancellation mail or have a telecom with your concerned sales executive with whom you have confirmed the package earlier.

Will I get refund if I don't use any of my booked services during the tour??

Unfortunately No. In case you have not utilized any of your pre-booked services like Transport, Meals, Stay etc we shall not be responsible for it. No refunds are made for un-availed services.

What if one of my scheduled activities is cancelled due to weather conditions?

In the event that a scheduled activity during your stay is cancelled due to weather conditions, we will offer alternatives (if possible) at no additional costs or may reschedule your itinerary.
